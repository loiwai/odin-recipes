Simply Delicious Blueberry Pie:

Tranquil depths

Ingredients

  • 2 prepared pie crusts (store-bought or homemade)
  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter (cut into small pieces)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F (200Â°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together blueberries, sugar, flour, lemon juice, lemon zest, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Place one pie crust in a pie dish and pour the blueberry mixture into it.
  4. Dot the top with pieces of butter.
  5. Cover with the second pie crust, seal the edges, and cut slits for ventilation.
  6. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the crust is golden and the filling is thick and bubbly.
  7. Allow the pie to cool before serving.
